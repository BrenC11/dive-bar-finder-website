FAQ

Last call for questions.

How do I find real dive bars near me?
Open Dive Bar Finder, allow location access, choose the Dive bars filter and adjust strictness. You can compare nearby options in the list or on the map. Learn how to find dive bars near you →
Can I find punk, goth, rock and metal bars?
Yes. The app includes filters for punk, gothic, rock, metal and unique or alternative bars, which you can use separately or together. Learn how to find rock and metal bars →
How can I avoid tourist-trap bars?
Look beyond distance alone. Compare the venue description, photos, reviews, Dive Score and category evidence before choosing. Learn how to find hidden local bars →
Can I use Dive Bar Finder while travelling?
That is one of its core uses. Open the nearby map in a new city, check the venue details, save promising options and get directions. Learn how to find dives while travelling →
Does the app show bars that are open now?
Venue results can show open or closed status, with fuller opening-hour information on venue detail pages. Hours can change, so confirm before a long journey. Learn more about choosing a nearby bar →
Is Dive Bar Finder free?
Dive Bar Finder is free to download from the App Store and includes in-app purchases. It requires iOS 17.0 or later. View Dive Bar Finder on the App Store →
